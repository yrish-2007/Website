Welcome to RNHS!

The school is currently implementing a K to 12 basic education program offering both junior and senior high school. It is in accordance with the resolution that was ordered by the Department of Education (DepEd).

The school has HumSS strand, STEM strand, ABM strand and GAS under the Academic track as well as Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track which equips students with technical skills for its senior high school program.

What programs & courses does Rosales National High School offer?

Choosing the right program can help you set your future goals and visualize where you want to be. Whether you want to be an engineer, a teacher, an accountant, or you want to level up in your profession, making yourself informed with the right choices will surely back you up in the future.

Strands

STEM

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) strand is designed for students who have a strong passion for scientific discovery, technological advancements, and problem-solving.

ABM

The Accounting and Business Management (ABM) strand is perfect for students eager to explore finance, entrepreneurship, and corporate management.

HUMSS

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand is designed for students with a strong passion for literature, social studies, communication, philosophy, and the arts.

ICT

The ICT strand focuses on programming, networking, and software development, preparing students for careers in the ever-expanding IT sector.

HE

The Home Economics (HE) strand covers practical skills in cooking, sewing, budgeting, home management, and caregiving, preparing students for careers in hospitality, tourism, catering, and entrepreneurship.

SMAW

The Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) strand provides hands-on training in welding techniques, metal cutting, and fabrication, equipping students with skills for careers in construction, manufacturing, and shipbuilding.

GAS

The General Academic Strand (GAS) offers a flexible curriculum for students who are still exploring their career options, integrating various disciplines for a well-rounded education.
